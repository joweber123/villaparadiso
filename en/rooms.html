Our Rooms

Each of our simple, cozy rooms are thoughtfully decorated. Each has a private bathroom and a beautiful view over the Mediteranean sea.

Sleeping arrangements

There are single, twin, double and three bed rooms available with the possible addition of beds for children according to your needs.

Breakfast

A complimentary breakfast is included, whether you want to rest until late or leave early for an excursion; in the morning you will be greeted by the smell of coffee, fresh bread, eggs, delicious jams and homemade marmalade.
